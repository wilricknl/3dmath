\subsection{Exercise 2}

Rewrite the equation with basic algebra:

\begin{align*} 
4x + 7y &= 42 \\ 
7y &= 42 - 4x \\
y &= -\frac{4}{7}x + 6
\end{align*}

\noindent Find the slope and Y-intercept:

\begin{itemize}
	\item Y-intercept: $f(0)=0+6=6$
	\item Slope: $-\frac{4}{7}$
\end{itemize}
